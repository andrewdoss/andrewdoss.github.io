About

I currently work as a data scientist at bit.io, an early-stage, venture-backed SaaS startup where I split my time between product and growth analytics, tool development, and marketing.

Prior to bit.io, I worked in the core advanced analytics team at a large automotive enterprise, where I deployed solutions for problems ranging from warehouse optimization, to vehicle fleet reliability, to dynamic pricing.

Before pivoting into tech, I worked as an aerospace propulsion engineer. I started as a fluid dynamicist and later led the analytical aspects of the development of several novel aircraft systems that are in use today.
